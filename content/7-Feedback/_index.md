> Below are my personal evaluations and suggestions after participating in the First Cloud Journey program.

### General Evaluation

**1. Learning and Working Environment**
The environment was friendly, open, and supportive. Team members were willing to help whenever difficulties arose. The program could include more networking or team-building activities to strengthen connections among participants.

**2. Support from Mentors and Team Admins**
The mentors provided clear guidance and encouraged participants to conduct independent research and solve problems proactively. The team admins also supported participants effectively by providing documents, schedules, and necessary information.

**3. Relevance to My Field of Study**
The program was relevant to Information Technology and allowed me to apply knowledge learned at university. It also introduced me to cloud computing, AWS services, and system development.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**
Through the InboxIQ project, I improved my programming, teamwork, documentation, communication, research, and troubleshooting skills.

**5. Team Culture and Collaboration**
Team members treated one another respectfully and cooperated effectively. This supportive culture made the project implementation process more efficient.

**6. Support and Benefits for Participants**
The program provided learning materials, technical guidance, and opportunities to practise with AWS services. These resources were valuable for developing practical knowledge and experience.

---

### Additional Questions

* **What was the most satisfying part of the program?**
  The most satisfying part was participating in a practical project, applying my academic knowledge, and receiving support from mentors and team members.

* **What could the program improve?**
  The program could provide a clearer roadmap for each stage, define more specific evaluation criteria, and organize regular progress meetings between mentors and participants.

* **Would I recommend this program to my friends? Why?**
  Yes. I would recommend the program to students interested in cloud computing and AWS because it provides practical knowledge, teamwork experience, and opportunities to develop professional skills.

### Suggestions and Expectations

* **Suggestions for improving the program experience**
  The program could organize more technical workshops, project review sessions, experience-sharing activities, and career orientation sessions.

* **Would I like to continue participating in the future?**
  Yes. I would like to participate in advanced training programs or practical projects related to AWS and cloud computing in the future.

* **Additional feedback**
  I sincerely appreciate the First Cloud Journey team, mentors, and team admins for their support throughout the program. The knowledge and experience I gained will be an important foundation for my future studies and career development.
